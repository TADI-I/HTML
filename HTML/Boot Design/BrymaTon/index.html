About Us



"Brymaton Construction is a well-established building construction company, registered under the Companies Act Chapter 24:03 on the 21st of March 2018. It primarily focuses on civil construction, concrete works, concrete blocks, general building construction, and all renovation services, including plumbing and ceiling. The company also provides labour to other companies, offering services such as truck drivers, loaders, garden boys, and builders. Despite its relatively young age, we have managed to grow our revenue and market beyond expectations. We are fully geared to be a preferred construction service and labour provider in Zimbabwe and other Southern African countries. The company is 100% black-owned and draws its main functionaries from highly technical experiences. It is our desire to offer quality civil construction and general labour services. Our company’s operational management is in the capable hands of experienced professionals who apply their expertise to integrate our services with those of our clients in order to meet their requirements for an effective, high-quality service. Our existence is solely based on quality service and installations, and we subscribe to the principles of openness and accountability."
